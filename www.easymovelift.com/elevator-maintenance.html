According to state regulations, elevators must be certified and inspected on a regular basis. An elevator should be inspected at least four times each year. Remember the following points to understand how frequently your elevator has to be checked:

  • • If your elevator seems unsteady, it is likely due to maintenance.
  • • Proper maintenance is crucial for modern elevators as they have several components that might malfunction.
  • • Regular inspections identify potential faults and fix worn-out parts.
  • • Frequent maintenance and repair are necessary for most components, including wiring, buttons, lights, valves, and door locks, due to heavy footfall.
